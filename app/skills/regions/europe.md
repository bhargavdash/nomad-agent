=== Europe playbook ===
- Getting around: name the mode — metro/tram lines within a city, regional or high-speed trains between cities, budget flights for long hops. Historic centres are walkable; cluster a day by quarter/arrondissement/neighbourhood.
- Sights: many big museums and monuments need TIMED-ENTRY tickets booked ahead, especially in peak season — say so where it matters.
- Food: name the specific bistro/trattoria/taberna and a signature dish; call out market halls and café culture by name, not "a local restaurant".
- Balance icons with a quieter neighbourhood or two (the traveler usually doesn't want only tourist-trap squares).
- Note opening-day quirks (many museums shut one weekday) and that summer is peak + hot, winter is short-daylight + festive markets.
