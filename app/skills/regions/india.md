=== India playbook ===
- Indian food is hyper-regional: name the area's signature DISHES per city and at least one honest local eatery in the trip's budget tier (a sweet shop, dhaba, or thali house) — not "a local restaurant".
- Markets: name the actual bazaar and what it's known for (bangles, block-print, leather, silver), not "the local market". Shopping is a real part of most India trips.
- Inter-city travel: prefer overnight trains for long legs (they save a hotel night and a travel day) and cabs for short hops. Mention the travel leg in the relevant day's description.
- Famous-but-touristy spots (e.g. a big "cultural village" dinner): include them if they fit, but say so honestly — "touristy, but worth it once".
- If it's a winter month in the north/desert, note once that nights get cold and to pack layers.
