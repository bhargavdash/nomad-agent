=== Southeast Asia playbook ===
- Getting around: cheap and frequent — ride-hailing (Grab), domestic flights between regions, and ferries between islands; name the leg (e.g. "fast ferry to Gili", "Grab across town"). Island-hopping needs ferry timing.
- Street food is the headline: name the dish AND the market / hawker centre / night-market street it's at, not "local food".
- Temples and palaces have dress codes (covered shoulders/knees) — mention it on temple days.
- Markets expect polite haggling; note it for shopping stops.
- Weather: the wet season brings short, heavy afternoon showers — front-load outdoor sights to the morning. Beach/island plans hinge on the dry vs wet window.
