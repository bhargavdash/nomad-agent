=== Food & markets focus ===
- This trip is explicitly about local food and shopping — treat them as first-class content, not afterthoughts.
- For EACH city, the day's `highlights` MUST include: at least one specific dish to eat (named), and — when shopping matters — what to buy + the named market to buy it at (e.g. "Bandhani dupattas at Bapu Bazaar").
- Name at least one honest local eatery per city in the budget tier (thali house, sweet shop, dhaba) rather than a generic "local restaurant".
- Prefer authentic local spots over touristy buffets unless the traveler asked otherwise; if a popular spot is touristy, say so honestly.
